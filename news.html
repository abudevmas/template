Latest News Articles

Date: March 15, 2024

The Community Board has unveiled several new initiatives aimed at improving neighborhood engagement and sustainability. These include a community recycling program, monthly neighborhood clean-up days, and an enhanced community garden project.

Residents are encouraged to participate in upcoming planning meetings. The first meeting will be held at the Community Center on March 25th at 6 PM.

Date: March 5, 2024

After months of construction, the community park renovation is finally complete! The park now features new playground equipment, upgraded walking trails, new picnic areas, and improved lighting for evening activities.

A grand reopening ceremony will be held this Saturday at 10 AM. All community members are invited to celebrate this milestone.

Date: February 28, 2024

Our local library has been awarded a $50,000 grant to expand digital resources and technology access. This funding will be used to upgrade computer labs, purchase new e-books, and offer digital literacy programs for all ages.

Starting in April, the library will offer free computer classes and expanded access to online learning platforms.

Date: February 20, 2024

The Community Hub is launching a Neighborhood Watch program in partnership with local law enforcement. This program aims to increase community safety through awareness, communication, and collaboration.

Volunteer training sessions will begin next month. Interested residents can sign up at the Community Center or online.

Date: February 15, 2024

Mark your calendars! The Annual Spring Festival is scheduled for April 20th, 2024. This year's festival will feature local vendors, live music, food trucks, children's activities, and much more.

Vendor applications are now open. If you're interested in participating, contact the Community Events Committee by March 10th.
